# Privacy Policy

**Markflow** is a native iOS markdown reader and editor.

## What we collect

Nothing. Markflow does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What the app does

- Reads `.md`, `.markdown`, and `.mdown` files you explicitly open from Files, Mail, Safari, the share sheet, or other apps.
- Renders them locally on your device using a sandboxed `WKWebView`.
- Optionally lets you edit the content in memory and export a copy via the system share sheet.

## What the app does not do

- No analytics or telemetry.
- No advertising.
- No accounts, no login, no cloud sync.
- No network requests. The renderer is a local HTML file with a Content Security Policy that blocks all remote resources.
- No access to your photos, contacts, location, microphone, or camera.

## Files on your device

Markflow only reads files you explicitly hand to it. The original file is never modified — edits happen in memory until you export a copy.

## Children

Markflow is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will live at the same URL with a new "last updated" date below.
